Ol pejeta conservancy

The ol pejeta conservancy is a 360km 2 not-for-profit wildlife conservancy in central kenya(laikipia county). Ol pejeta is situated on the equator west of Nanyuki,between the foothills of the Aberdare and Mount Kenya. It conserves wildlife and generate income through wildlife tourism. Ol pejeta is the conservancy to be. It contains the largest population of black rhino in east africa and the last 2 remaining northern white rhino in the world. if you visit Ol pejeta you will be welcomed by a black rhino near the ol pejeta entrance that loves people and is actually used to people hence making your safari memorable and captivating. Ol pejeta is one of the most protected conservancy in the world with sophisticated drones,piper PA-18 super cub light aircraft,armed anti poaching rangers and specialy trained bloodhounds and belgian malinois dogs to track and attack poachers respectively. Ol pejeta also has its "magnificent" 5 animals it boosts namely:

1.African wild dog

African wild dogs are one of the most spectacular but critically Endangered african animals. They have many names for example the cape hunting dogs,painted wolves or african painted dogs. In the past,wild dogs ranged from deserts to mountainous habitats but now they are extinct in majority of the habitats they thrived. Now large populations exists in about 6 countries in africa for example Tanzania and Botswana. Kenya wild dog population is recovering from canine distemper and rabies diseases that almost wiped the whole population of wild dogs in kenya. 2.Grevy's zebra

The Grevy zebra is also one of the most endangered africa animal in africa. It is very different from the common zebra with beautiful narrow close-set black and white patterns. It is also the biggest zebra species in Africa. This zebra species is found in Kenya and ethiopia and luckily,ol pejeta hosts many of this majestic zebras.

3.Jackson's hartebeest

ol pejeta also hosts the rare and stunning Jackson's hartebeest. It is a cross breed between the two species of hartebeest(lelwel and the coke's hartebeest).it is mostly found in kenya and uganda and ranges from dry savanna,open plains and wooded grasslands.

4.Bat eared fox

black eared fox are mostly found in african savannas. it has large ears which are very crucial to them for thermoregulation. it weighs about 3 to 5.3 kg and has a shoulder height of about 30-40cm. it ranges in most southern and eastern parts of africa though they look like mammal hunters,they actually eat small bugs lie termites,spiders,grasshoppers and beetles. They rarely eat mammals. Ol pejeta has a small population and if lucky,you will be able to spot this cute furry creature.

5.Oryx

Ol pejeta also has the majestic and beautiful Oryx. This majestic creatures are normally found in arid and semi arid areas from africa to the arabian peninsula. They can survive without water for many days. This animal lives in heard of up to 600 animal. With long sharp horns,they can easily defend themselves against predators and are known to kill lions. They calves can run with the heard after a few hours after being born. Oryx is a must see animal in ol pejeta.

Ol pejeta also hosts more than 300 species of birds and very many herbivore species including lions hence it is a place to be.
